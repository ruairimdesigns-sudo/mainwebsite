Getting to know me

Ruairi Madine is my full name - pronounced like brewery, but without the 'b' - originally from Kent.

I pride myself in being flexible and dynamic in new situations and environments - I am able to change my approach, efficiently working with clients and designers alike to get the most out of myself and each project. This carries through to my design ethic, as I have a variety of different skills and styles that I can work in. I am also confident in my broad understanding of what it takes to communicate a message, from understanding the core of a client, to discovering what is best for them. I also feel as though I am able to communicate a complex idea effectively in a much more accessible form. Above all else I have a constant desire to consistently improve, learn and develop my skill set and mindset as a designer.
