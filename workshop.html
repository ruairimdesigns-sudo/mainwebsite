Workshop Card Design

This was 3 decks of cards designed by myself with content created in partnership with another designer at HKD - with the intent to create play, challenge preconceptions and spark new ideas. These cards were created to be used in a workshop session with a large science centre in England, as they were imagining the possibilities of their new areas. Three distinct areas with distinct differences were designed to then be shuffled up, forcing the participants in the workshop to question what they thought they needed from these new spaces and what they could become.
*As the project is on-going I'm unable to note the name of the centre.
