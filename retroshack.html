Retro Shack Branding and T-Shirt Production

Retro Shack is a company that sells vintage clothes on Depop. With a young demographic, the created brand had to speak to them and the streetwear culture that they participated in. Similarily, the illustration style took the same direction. The t-shirts themsleves were hand screen-printed by myself.
