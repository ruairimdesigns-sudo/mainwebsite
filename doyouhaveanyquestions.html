Do You Have Any Questions?

A publication based around the theme of conspiracy theories - exploring why people are so quick to believe in them. Within this I try to understand what this says about the human psyche and modern society in the age of social media. In response to this I used a mixture of imagery/typography created using ink and redacting typography through the same methods.

Client/Partner:

Self-Directed

Project Info:

Long-form publication, screen printed cover, layout design, illustration, 6' by 9' publication

Wrap around cover that's revealed once heated up through touch
__

Heat-reactive screen printing ink cover that was then perfect bound by hand
__

Hand rendered typography to reflect the emotions of a conspiracy theorist
__

Free flowing ink illustrations that contrast the neat and uniform typography
__

Found imagery that was treated and edited to create collage-like sections
