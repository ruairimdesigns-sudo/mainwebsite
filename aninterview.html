An Interview with an Architect

Russell Stevens is a practicing architect and educator, with decades of experience. Talking to him about his current project - the swimming pool for the upcoming Commonwealth Games - and his general career, we produced this publication. We aimed at representing the profession of architecture. You can see this through the grid like lines, the professional hardback format and the clean, minimal design that made use of negative space. Most importantly we aimed to show the building up of imagery and layouts that is involved with architecture.

Created with Dan Schenck & Rachel Bonner.
