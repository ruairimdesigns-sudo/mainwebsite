Dysfunction & People

Whilst studying the functionality of people and how design could be worked into this, I created this short presentation. Using a mix of found imagery/animation, presenting my own work on the subject and those that inspired me I aimed to illustrate my own voiceover and keep the audience interested throughout. In addition to this I used styalised typography in order to create transitions and more simple type that visualised the subject I was talking about.
