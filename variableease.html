Variable Ease

A speculative design project which aimed to imagine how design and typography could be altered to suit the individual, easpecially those with physical or mental disabilities. This project was spawned out of an interest in the countless ways variable type could be used and I visualised how variable typefaces could help - among other aspects of design - make type accesible for everyone. The logo and brand reflects this adaptive nature of variable type and shows exactly what the idea does for people.
