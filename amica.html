Amica

Amica is a Bristolian local upcoming band that two other graphic designers and I created a brand and typographic music video for. Creating a coherent brand meant a consistent design approach used throughout the process and appearance of all aspects of the design created for them - the music video, logo, poster, CD case and flip book. The video was created through a process that mixed analogue and digital. It involved creating original typography, projectors and movement - which we then edited and played with for the outcome you can see below.

Created with Dan Schenck & Rachel Bonner.
