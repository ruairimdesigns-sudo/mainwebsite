Animated Design

This is a group of different animated pieces I've produced. One being a short series of animated posters with the aim to change the perspective of the user to understand a variety of emotions and disabilities. This was done using Cinema4D typography and by augmented reality which alters the perspective of the viewer, as they can begin to perceive an understanding of the world that others may see or feel.
