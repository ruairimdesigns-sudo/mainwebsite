Minirig & UWE Competition-Winning Speaker Design

A selected design that won in competition with the entirety of the University of the West of England. The brief was to celebrate the unique culture within Bristol in the form of a speaker design that would then be produced and handed out as award prizes. To solve this I decided to focus on the wide variety of people that make up Bristol and truly give it life, by embodying them through a commercial and energetic style of illustration.

Client/Partner:

Minirig & UWE Alumni
