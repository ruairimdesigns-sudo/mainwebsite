HKD Brand Adjustment

Working closely with everyone at HKD we adjusted the brand to suit the changing market during covid-19, which meant working towards the new requirements of both their Chinese market and their English market. I created a series of project sheet layouts which embodied all of the work HKD had done for the past year. Additionally I created new cv layouts, case studies of their past star exhibits and - working closely with Kate \kneale, the director - branded, filmed and edited a new Instagram series. All of this was neccessary in HKD finding future work.

Houghton & Kneale Design Ltd.
