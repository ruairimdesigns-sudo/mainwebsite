Internship at Green Goblet

Undergoing an internship over 10 weeks, I produced multiple cup designs - as well as a variety of other design work. These cups and displayed the company's environmentally friendly ethos. There are over 100,00 of these cups printed and are in constant circulation around a plethora of events across the UK, including Glastonbury.

Client/Partner:

Green Goblet

Project Info:

Product design, 3D mockups, interacting with clients, illustration, poster design
