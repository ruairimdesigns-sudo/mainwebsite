Fiery Owl Brand Creation

A brand identity was discovered for this new start up marketing company through working with the client and going off their original ideas. The owl was chosen to represent wisdom, sharp decision making and to be used as a future character in branding. The fiery element was introduced subtly through the brand colour choices and expressed the energy and passion Fiery Owl had.
